WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. People with technical backgrounds come together, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch — these generally take shape in the form of websites, mobile apps, and robots. Hackathons give participants the chance to develop skills and products that have real-world uses

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

DelhiHacks’ hackathons are geared towards High School and College students. However, any student who is in school or recently graduated may participate! (no matter the age or location)

DO I NEED A TEAM?

One team required 2-6 people. Teams can be made up of 2-6 people. For those who don’t have a team but want one, there will be a team building workshop after opening session!

WHY SHOULD I JOIN?

With five different tracks including environment, health, education, inequality, and poverty, participants will be able to work with other youths all over the world to solve various issues. Through this supportive community, attendees advance their programming/technical knowledge.
